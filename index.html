About Me

Hi! My name is Ronald Greer(sometimes I go by Trey) and I'm a front end web developer. I'm a student at Ripley High School in Ripley, West Virginia, who hopes to do great things in the future in the tech field. I love a good challenge, especially when it comes to programming, not only do you learn immensely from it but it feels much more rewarding.

My love for all things programming began when I entered the West Virginia Coding Club in the 6th grade. On my first day there, I learned to write a small line of code in Java that would display my name in the console. While it wasn't the most amazing piece of code, it captivated me on what could be possible with programming. Naturally, I pushed myself to learn more.

Some of the coding languages that I am fluent in are: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
